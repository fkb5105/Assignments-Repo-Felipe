Videogame Analysis

Vanish by ZeroDigitZ

Features:

  • - 30 Minute gameplay
  • - Mystery & Horror RPG
  • - Compatible with MacOS and Windows
  • - Original Soundtrack
  • - Puzzles & Minigames

Gameplay:

The gameplay is very simple throughout the game. The movements are forward, backwards, left and right and you can either click with the mouse where you want to go or use the arrows at the bottom right of the keyboard. To move forward in conversations or character interactions you can press the space bar or enter. Selecting options is also pretty easy, you can click on them or just move with the arrows through the options and press enter when you choose it.

Graphics:

The graphics in vanish are what is known as 2D sprite tile-based graphical style, which I think are a great choice for these type of games. Other exploration-adventure games such as Terraria also have these graphics because it makes the map easy to understand. Every asset, NPC or room in Vanish is well designed, there are a lot of colors that make the game more appealing.

Sound:

The videogame includes an original, unique soundtrack that is probably one of the best features in the game. Since the moment you open it a misterious and scary music starts playing and immediately immerse yourself in the game. Everytime you go through a door or something unexpected is happening, like an alarm going off or the floor breaking there is a specific noise in the background. "The Flock" has an actual voice, that seems to be modified digitally to give a more dramatic effect.
